Andrew Residence On-Call Staffing

Currently in progress. Collaborating with a team of 4 to build a full stack scheduling application for on-call staff at the Andrew Residence. Utilizing agile methodologies and participating in daily standups to communicate progress, plans and impediments. Administrators are able to post shift openings for on-call staff. They also have the ability to accept picked up shifts, send out urgent notifications and view upcoming shifts. On-call staff may bid on open shifts, request partial shifts and view their upcoming shifts.

Technologies Used: PostgreSQL, Express, AngularJS, Node.js, JavaScript, HTML 5, CSS 3, Angular MaterialJS, Moment.js, Passport, Postico, Heroku, Git, GitHub, VirtualBox, Plivo, NodeMailer
